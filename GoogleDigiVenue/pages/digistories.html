Not a social media package. Not a posting service. DigiStories is our visual engine that runs your Google page, Instagram presence, and customer reviews to make your venue clearly active and trusted when families search.

01 · Google & Search

Google Presence & Maps

Most families open Google before they ever contact a venue. They search "banquet hall near me" or type your name directly. What they see in the next ten seconds decides whether they call you or skip to the next venue. We make sure what they see builds immediate trust.

◆
Local search, set up for your area

We optimize your map coordinates and local search presence for your exact region, so families nearby see your banquet first.

◆
Operating hours & details

We keep your holiday hours, Ganpati schedules, and capacity parameters updated weekly so families don't hit dead ends.

◆
Direct calls and WhatsApp enquiries

We set up clean links for direct calls, map directions, and WhatsApp chats, removing listing portal commissions.

What we do every week

Boring, repetitive, reliable tasks that keep a banquet hall looking active and trustworthy month after month:

  • Verify Google listing accuracy — map location, phone number, and address.
  • Monitor and reply politely to every new customer review in your venue's voice.
  • Post 2–3 fresh photos captured from the previous weekend's actual setup.
  • Update seasonal details (wedding dates, festival bookings, corporate parties).
  • Maintain clear answers to common customer Q&As (parking capacity, catering options).
02 · Visuals & Social

Real Photography & Reels

Indian families decide with their eyes. If your last photo is a blurry mobile shot from a year ago, they assume you aren't booking. Our team visits your venue on event days to capture the exact decor, mandap setups, and florals that families look for.

◆
Real photos on event days

We don't ask your staff for blurry phone shots. Our team visits your banquet during setups and functions to capture the actual event.

◆
Mandap, floral & catering details

High-quality close-ups of stage lighting, entrance floral arches, and active buffet setups — the details families inspect online.

◆
Real weddings on Reels

We edit and publish weekly reels of real functions at your venue. No Canva templates, no stock graphics, no generic holiday cards.

03 · Reputation

The Trust & Reviews Engine

A brochure makes promises, but reviews build trust. When a family checks your venue name, they inspect your recent reviews. We set up simple, post-event follow-up loops to make sure your happy hosts leave detailed, positive reviews that help win the next client.

◆
Post-event feedback loops

We follow up with families after their functions in a polite, respectful way to gather reviews while their positive experience is fresh.

◆
A steady trail of trust

Instead of reviews stopping months ago, we build a regular flow of reviews so the page always looks active and trustworthy.

◆
Handling negative reviews

If a client leaves a complaint, we don't post robotic template responses. We help you resolve the operational issue, and write a calm, professional reply.
