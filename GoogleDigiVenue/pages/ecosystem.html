The operating ecosystem

Five layers.
One ecosystem built for venues.

DigiVenue is not a single product. It is a set of interconnected layers — each built for a different part of running a banquet business. Most venues start with DigiStories. The rest grows with them, as they grow.

How the layers connect

Each layer builds on the one before it.

Visibility (DigiStories) brings the enquiries. The operational layer (SmartOS) converts and tracks them. The network (DigiNetwork) supports them. The commerce layer (DigiMarket) supplies them. And eventually, DigiShaadi puts your venue in front of families searching directly — without a middleman.

01 · The visibility layer

DigiStories

Banquet visibility transformation.

Instagram, Google presence, real event photography, reels, reviews and trust-building content — everything a venue needs to appear active and trusted during the modern family discovery journey. This is where every venue starts.

Launching now
02 · The operational layer

SmartOS

Venue management, built for India.

Enquiry tracking, booking management, calendar conflicts, payments, GST invoicing and vendor royalty tracking — built specifically for Indian banquet operators. Quiet, operational, and essential once the enquiries start coming.

Prototype stage
03 · The network layer

DigiNetwork

Hospitality relationship infrastructure.

Connecting banquet halls with trusted caterers, decorators, photographers and planners through relationship-based referrals — not commission-driven directories. When a family asks, you have an answer.

In development
04 · The commerce layer

DigiMarket

Hospitality supplies, better rates.

Sourcing decor, lighting, display materials and event supplies for banquet halls at better rates — through the DigiVenue network of trusted hospitality suppliers. Things your venue buys anyway, at fairer prices.

In development
05 · The discovery layer

DigiShaadi

Where families find trusted venues.

A venue-trust layer where families searching for Indian wedding banquets can find real venues, see real wedding photos and make informed decisions — without commission-driven listing portals taking a cut of every booking.

Concept stage
How we grow with you

Three years. One season at a time.

Trust online doesn't happen overnight. It comes from showing up consistently — month after month, season after season. Here is the order we add layers, and why that order matters.

Year 1 Layers 01 + 02 — DigiStories & SmartOS

Get visible. Get organised.

We fix your Google presence and start shooting real photos from your functions every event day. Alongside that, SmartOS begins tracking enquiries and bookings so nothing falls through a gap. By the end of the first season, families find an active, reviewed venue — and every enquiry is captured.

Year 2 Layer 03 — DigiNetwork

Build the right relationships.

More enquiries are coming in, and families are asking about decorators, caterers and photographers. DigiNetwork connects you to trusted vendors from the DigiVenue network — so when a family asks, you can offer a reliable answer, not an awkward pause.

Year 3+ Layers 04 + 05 — DigiMarket & DigiShaadi

Grow beyond visibility.

Your venue is well-known and well-reviewed. DigiMarket helps you source supplies at better rates through the network. And DigiShaadi — when it launches — gives your venue a place in a trust-first family discovery layer, where families come looking directly, without a commission portal in between.
