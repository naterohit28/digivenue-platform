# THE WEDDING INDUSTRY CHANGED
## The Decision Maker and Buying Journey Have Shifted

### Earlier
* Parents and grandparents decided.
* Families trusted word-of-mouth recommendations.

### Today
* Gen Z and young couples make the decisions.
* Families trust digital research (Google, Instagram, Reviews, Websites).

# GUEST COUNTS REDUCED, EXPECTATIONS EXPLODED
## The Industry Became More Complex, Not Smaller

### Earlier (Simple & Large)
* 1,000+ Guests
* 1 Single Function

### Today (Boutique & Multi-day)
* 250 Guests
* 5–7 Ancillary Functions (Engagement, Mehendi, Haldi, Cocktail, Sangeet, Wedding, Reception)

# THE MONEY MOVED
## Spending Shifting to Premium Spaces and Micro-Events

### Where Premium Spending is Going
* Destination Weddings, Resorts, and Villas
* High-end Experiences & Production (Decor, Light, Sound)

### The Rise of Ancillary Events
* Birthdays, Baby Showers, Anniversaries, Namkaran
* Demand is high, but spending patterns are completely different.
