Industry shift

The venue industry
has changed.

The wedding market didn't slow down. It evolved.

While operators run properties the way they did ten years ago, customer behaviour has shifted permanently — opening a wide gap between how families research and how venues sell.

DigiStories exists to close that gap.

Post-COVID realities
  • Smaller guest counts mean highly selective booking behaviour.
  • Destination weddings are pulling demand away from local venues.
  • Fragmented revenues — décor and catering are increasingly decentralised.
  • Rising overheads — higher rents and constant upgrade pressure.
  • Digital pre-filtering — families finish their research online before calling anyone.
