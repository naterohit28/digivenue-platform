Worth thinking about

You built the banquet.
The first impression online shouldn't belong to someone else.

Families still look for your banquet by name — on Google, on Instagram, asking relatives. But often that search goes through a listing website or an agent first. You did the hard work of building the banquet over the years. That first impression online should be yours.

Listing websites have their place — they do help families find banquets. The problem is leaning on them completely. When your own Google page looks empty, families end up booking through a middleman, and you pay commission to reach people who were already looking for you.

This isn't about fighting those websites. It's about your own banquet looking as good online as it does in person — so families can come to you directly.

We haven't launched widely yet. But the work already runs — quietly, every week — for two banquet halls in Navi Mumbai. Real functions photographed, Google pages kept active, families finding a banquet that looks alive when they search.
