Industry Shift

The Venue Industry
Has Changed.

The wedding market did not slow down. It evolved.

While operators continue running properties the same way they did ten years ago, customer behavior has shifted permanently. This has created a massive gap between how customers research and buy, and how venues sell.

DigiStories exists to close that gap.

Post-COVID Realities:
  • • Reduced Guest Counts: Tighter guest lists translate to highly selective booking behavior.
  • • Destination Weddings: Competition from destination weddings has accelerated.
  • • Fragmented Ancillary Revenues: Event decor and catering revenues are increasingly decentralized.
  • • Escalating Overheads: Operators face higher rents and constant property upgrade pressures.
  • • Digital Pre-filtering: Families complete their visual research online before contacting any venue.
The Margin Squeeze

The Perfect Storm Facing Venue Operators

Customers Want More

  • • More functions & pre-wedding shoots
  • • Immersive experiences and live lighting setups
  • • High personalization options for layout and catering
  • • Immediate social proof, reels, and video coverage

Operators Face More Pressure

  • • Escalating land lease rates and rents
  • • Frequent property renovation and upkeep demands
  • • More aggressive local competitor pricing
  • • Rising marketing, client acquisition, and staff costs

Result: Margins are under severe pressure even when demand exists.
