How DigiVenue works

Five parts.
One system, built for banquets.

DigiVenue isn't a single product. It's five connected parts — each built for a different side of running a banquet hall. Most banquets start with DigiStories. The rest grows with you, as you grow.

How they connect

Each part builds on the one before it.

DigiStories brings the enquiries. SmartOS handles the day-to-day running. DigiNetwork connects you to trusted vendors. DigiMarket helps you buy supplies for less. And one day, DigiShaadi will put your banquet in front of families searching directly — with no middleman taking a cut.

How we grow with you

Three years. One season at a time.

Trust online doesn't happen overnight. It comes from showing up steadily — month after month, season after season. Here's the order we add each part, and why the order matters.

Year 1 DigiStories & SmartOS

Get seen. Get organised.

We fix your Google page and start shooting real photos at your functions. Alongside that, SmartOS begins tracking enquiries and bookings, so nothing slips through. By the end of the first season, families find an active, well-reviewed banquet — and every enquiry is captured.

Year 2 DigiNetwork

Build the right relationships.

More enquiries are coming in, and families are asking about decorators, caterers and photographers. DigiNetwork connects you to trusted vendors from the network — so when a family asks, you have a reliable answer, not an awkward pause.

Year 3+ DigiMarket & DigiShaadi

Grow beyond visibility.

Your banquet is well known and well reviewed. DigiMarket helps you buy supplies at better rates through the network. And DigiShaadi, when it launches, gives your banquet a place where families come looking directly — with no commission portal in between.
