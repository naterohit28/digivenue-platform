Where we are today

We haven't launched yet. So we won't pretend we have.

You won't find made-up numbers or borrowed success stories here. DigiVenue is still at the early stage — running quietly for two real banquet halls in Navi Mumbai. Here's exactly what we're doing for them, and what we'll put on this page the moment the results are real.

Running now · our first banquets

Two real banquets. Live, every week.

Before showing this to anyone, we wanted to run the full service on real banquets — not slides. These two in Navi Mumbai are where we're doing exactly that.

Vashi, Navi Mumbai

IES MLA Hall

A known community banquet in Vashi. We are documenting its real functions, rebuilding its Google presence, and keeping its page active week after week.

Jui Nagar, Navi Mumbai

Swayambhu Ganesh Sabhagruh

A respected sabhagruh in Jui Nagar. Same approach — real event photography, steady Google and Instagram activity, and reviews followed up after every function.

01 / What we found

Good banquets. Real functions every weekend. But online, both looked quiet — old photos, thin Google pages, almost no recent activity. The kind of page a family scrolls past without realising the banquet is busy and well run.

02 / What we do every week

Our team attends their functions and captures the real setups — the mandap, the lighting, the buffet, the guests. Those photos and reels go up on Google and Instagram the same week, sized and tagged for local search.

03 / How trust gets built

After each function, families are asked — politely, over WhatsApp — for an honest review. Slowly, the page stops looking abandoned and starts looking like what it actually is: a banquet that hosts real weddings, every week.

04 / What we're watching

Reviews, photo count, map views, direction requests, calls from Google. We track all of it from each venue's own Google Business Profile. When the numbers are real and the owners are happy to share them, they will appear here — with their names on them.
