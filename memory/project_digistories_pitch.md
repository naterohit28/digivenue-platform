The DigiVenue master pitch deck is built to pitch **venue operators** on adopting DigiStories.

**Why:** Rohit wants to sell the DigiStories concept to venue owners/operators — not raise funding or find channel partners.

**How to apply:** Keep language practical and operator-centric (margins, enquiries, ownership, cost). Avoid investor language (TAM, runway, ARR). Every slide should speak to a venue owner's daily reality.
