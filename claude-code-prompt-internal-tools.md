## Sales Tool + Growth Bible: From Marketing Audit → Venue Growth Diagnostic System

## PART 0 — STRATEGIC VISION

You are upgrading two existing internal tools:

1. **`digistories-sales-tool-v2.html`** — Used by a DigiVenue consultant during a live 20–30 minute call with a banquet hall owner, caterer, or venue operator. Currently feels like a marketing audit. Must become a **Venue Growth Diagnostic System**.
2. **`venue-growth-bible-engine.html`** — A 21-chapter internal strategy reference used by the delivery team. Must become a **Venue Growth Operating Manual**.

### The six questions every field must help answer

This is the master editorial test. If a field on the sales tool does not help answer one of these six questions, **remove it**:

1. Why is this venue not growing?
2. Where is revenue leaking?
3. Where are inquiries getting lost?
4. What should be fixed first?
5. Which DigiVenue solution fits best?
6. How likely is this venue to become a customer?

### The diagnostic outcome

By the end of a consultation, the consultant must have determined:
- **DigiStories Fit** (Visibility & Conversion problems)
- **SmartOS Fit** (Operational problems)
- **Revenue Opportunity** (₹ figure, realistic ranges only)
- **Close Probability** (Low / Medium / High)

And critically — sometimes the answer is **Neither**. A consultant who can say *"fix X yourself first, come back in 6 months"* protects DigiVenue from bad-fit customers who churn.

### The owner's experience

The venue owner should leave the call thinking:

> *"Nobody has ever analyzed my business this deeply."*

This means the tool must feel like a **Bain consultant** or a **business doctor** — not a marketing agency, not a CRM, not a social media audit.

### The six pillars

Every diagnostic question maps to one of six business pillars:

1. **Visibility** — Can customers find this venue?
2. **Trust** — Once found, do customers trust it?
3. **Conversion** — When inquiries come, what happens?
4. **Operations** — Can this venue manage growth?
5. **Revenue** — Is the venue maximizing its revenue potential?
6. **Leadership** *(internal only)* — Is management capable of growth?

**DigiStories** solves Visibility, Trust, and Conversion.
**SmartOS** solves Operations and Conversion.
**Both** improve Revenue.
**Leadership** determines Close Probability.

### Two different audiences, one file

- **Sales tool on-screen UI** → seen by the consultant → internal-team language acceptable
- **Sales tool printable "Venue Health Report"** → given to the owner (45–70 yr non-tech Indian banquet operator) → plain, calm, respectful language
- **Growth Bible** → internal-only → team language fine throughout

### Constraints

- **Do not redesign visually.** Same fonts, colours, layout, file structure.
- **Do not break the JSON bridge** between the two tools.
- **Do not invent statistics, testimonials, or claims.**
- **Show realistic ranges. Avoid fake projections.**
