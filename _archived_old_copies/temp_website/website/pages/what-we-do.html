What we do

We quietly run your Google, Instagram and reviews.

Not a social media package. Not a posting service. We're a small team that handles the online side of your banquet hall the way a good operations manager would — every week, without you having to follow up.

01 · Google & Search

DigiVenue Core

Most families open Google before they open WhatsApp. They search "banquet hall in Chembur" or type your banquet name directly. What they see in the next ten seconds decides whether they call you or call the next banquet on the list. We make sure what they see looks right.

◆
Local search, done properly

We build your presence for your exact locality — Chembur, Dadar, Thane West, Vashi — so families nearby see you first when they search.

◆
A fresh review every 2–3 weeks

After every function, we follow up with the family and bring back an honest review. Over a year, your page tells a real story.

◆
Direct calls and WhatsApp enquiries

Your Google page, your map pin, your phone number — set up so a family can call or WhatsApp you in one tap, with no middleman.

What we do every week

A small, repeating list. Boring. Reliable. Exactly the kind of work that keeps a banquet looking active month after month:

  • Check your Google page is correct — hours, phone number, address, map pin.
  • Read every new review and reply to it — politely, in your voice.
  • Post 2–3 fresh photos from the previous weekend's function.
  • Update the season — wedding months, Ganpati, corporate year-end — as it shifts.
  • Answer the questions families ask on Google (capacity, parking, rates) so they don't have to call to find out.
02 · Photos & Reels

DigiStories

Families decide with their eyes. If your latest photo is a blurry phone shot from two seasons ago, they assume the banquet is sitting empty. We send our own team to your functions, so the photos on your page show what your banquet actually looks like on a Saturday night.

◆
We're there on event days

Our team comes to your banquet during weddings and receptions — quietly, without disturbing the family — to capture the decor, the food, and the moments that matter.

◆
The details families look for

The mandap floral work, the entrance lighting, the buffet stations, the seated guests. The things a family scrolls for when they're deciding whether your banquet is the one.

◆
Ready for Google and Instagram

Every photo is colour-corrected, sized for mobile, and tagged so it shows up when families nearby are searching. You don't have to do anything.

03 · Enquiry handling

SmartOS

Getting the enquiry is the easy part. If a family messages on WhatsApp at 9 in the evening and your team replies the next morning, the booking is already gone. SmartOS is the small set of habits and templates we set up with your sales desk so every enquiry actually turns into a site visit.

◆
WhatsApp replies within minutes

A simple tracker so no enquiry sits unanswered. Most are replied to in under 10 minutes — including evenings, which is when families actually message.

◆
A clean rate sheet for WhatsApp

Your packages, capacity and menu — in a single, well-designed PDF that looks good on a phone. No more sending blurry photos of printed sheets.

◆
Scripts your manager can actually use

Plain-language scripts for the common questions — rates, dates, parking, menu changes — and for taking the family on a smooth site visit.
